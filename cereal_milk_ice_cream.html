Cereal Milk Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 500g 2% milk
  • 150g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 70g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 60g Kellog's Corn Flakes Cereal
  • 3g vanilla bean paste

Instructions

  1. Steep the cereal in milk for 25–30 minutes at room temperature, then strain and press to extract as much flavor as possible. Discard soggy solids.
  2. Whisk together sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder in a bowl.
  3. In a saucepan, combine the infused milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  4. Add vanilla bean paste and blend with immersion blender until smooth.
  5. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  7. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  8. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~210

Notes

This ice cream captures the nostalgic flavor of cereal-soaked milk with a creamy cinnamon finish. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -13°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
