Crème Brûlée Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 400g 2% milk
  • 200g heavy cream (33%)
  • 100g Rogers Natural Dark Brown Sugar
  • 60g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Brûléed Sugar Brittle

  • 30g granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. To make the brûléed sugar brittle, line a small baking sheet with parchment. In a dry pan over medium heat, melt the granulated sugar until it turns a deep amber color. Immediately pour onto the parchment and spread thin. Let cool completely, then break into small shards. Reserve to fold in post-churn.
  2. Whisk together brown sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder in a bowl.
  3. In a saucepan, combine milk and cream and bring to 60–65°C.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks. Temper with some of the warm dairy mixture, then return to the pot.
  5. Add dry mix and continue heating to 82–84°C while stirring constantly to form a custard base.
  6. Remove from heat and blend in vanilla bean paste and salt with an immersion blender until smooth.
  7. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  8. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  9. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  10. Fold in shards of cooled brûléed sugar brittle. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.
  11. Just before serving, scoop the ice cream into a shallow, freezer-safe dish. Sprinkle a thin, even layer of granulated sugar over the surface and brûlée using a kitchen torch until golden and crisp. Let sit 30–60 seconds to allow the sugar to harden before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~235

Notes

Inspired by the classic French dessert, this custard-style ice cream features deep caramelized brown sugar, egg yolks, and real vanilla bean paste. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Folded-in brûléed sugar brittle adds authentic texture and a caramelized crunch throughout. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -13°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
