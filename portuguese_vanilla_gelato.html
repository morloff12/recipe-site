Portuguese Vanilla Gelato

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 600g whole milk (3.25%)
  • 100g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 60g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • Zest of 1 lemon (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp ground cardamom (optional)
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk together sugar, skim milk powder, CMC powder, cinnamon, and cardamom in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  3. Add vanilla bean paste, lemon zest (if using), and salt. Blend with immersion blender until smooth.
  4. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  5. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  6. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  7. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~200

Notes

Inspired by Portuguese leite creme, this velvety vanilla gelato features a subtle chai twist from cinnamon and cardamom with a bright citrus finish. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -11°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
