Banana Nutella Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 475g 2% milk
  • 210g heavy cream (33%)
  • 95g granulated sugar
  • 55g corn syrup
  • 38g skim milk powder
  • 2.4g CMC powder
  • 3 large egg yolks (~50g)
  • 120g ripe banana purée
  • 110g Nutella hazelnut spread
  • 30g chopped roasted hazelnuts (optional, fold-in)
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk together granulated sugar, skim milk powder, CMC powder, and fine sea salt in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and the dry mix. Heat gently until steamy and fully dissolved.
  3. Whisk egg yolks in a separate bowl. Temper with some of the warm dairy, then return the mixture to the saucepan.
  4. Cook while stirring constantly until the custard thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Remove from heat. Add banana purée and Nutella, then blend thoroughly with an immersion blender until fully emulsified.
  6. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  7. Refrigerate for 12 hours to mature the base and fully hydrate stabilizers.
  8. Churn in a compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency, about 35–45 minutes.
  9. If using hazelnuts, fold them in gently after churning. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden at -18°C for at least 4 hours.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~245

Notes

A rich and creamy ice cream combining ripe banana purée with smooth Nutella, optionally finished with roasted hazelnuts. The banana enhances softness and body while complementing the chocolate-hazelnut base. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 7–10 days. Serve at -13°C for optimal texture.
