Pink Cotton Candy Ice Cream

A smooth, egg-free pink cotton candy ice cream formulated for a clear carnival-style flavour, creamy body, reliable scoopability, and stable texture after hard freezing. LorAnn Pink Cotton Candy Ice Cream Flavor Fountain provides the primary flavour, while a controlled amount of pink gel colouring creates a consistent family-friendly appearance. Use only the specified Ice Cream Flavor Fountain; LorAnn Super-Strength Cotton Candy Flavor requires a substantially different dosage.

Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato
Yield: Approximately 1000 g
Prep time: 25 minutes active
Cook time: 15 minutes
Additional time: Approximately 16 hours 40 minutes including ageing, churning, and hardening

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
2% milk 557.5 g
33% heavy cream 200 g
Granulated sugar 125 g
Light corn syrup 55 g
Skim milk powder 48 g
LorAnn Pink Cotton Candy Ice Cream Flavor Fountain 12 g
CMC powder 1.5 g
Fine sea salt 0.8 g
Pink gel food colouring 0.2 g

Instructions

  1. Combine the granulated sugar, skim milk powder, CMC powder, and fine sea salt in a dry bowl. Whisk thoroughly until the stabilizer is evenly dispersed and no visible clumps remain.
  2. Add the milk and heavy cream to a saucepan. Begin heating over medium-low heat while stirring continuously with a flexible spatula.
  3. When the dairy is warm, gradually rain in the dry mixture while whisking continuously. Do not add it all at once, as concentrated CMC can form persistent gel particles.
  4. Add the light corn syrup and continue stirring until the mixture is completely uniform.
  5. Heat the base to 60–65°C. Hold it within this range for approximately two minutes while stirring constantly. Do not boil.
  6. Remove the saucepan from the heat and blend the base with an immersion blender until completely smooth, keeping the blender head submerged to avoid incorporating excess air.
  7. Transfer the base to a clean container and cool it rapidly over an ice bath. Stir periodically until the temperature falls below 10°C.
  8. Add the LorAnn Pink Cotton Candy Ice Cream Flavor Fountain and pink gel food colouring. Blend briefly until the flavour and colour are distributed evenly.
  9. Cover the base directly against the surface and refrigerate for 12 hours. Do not shorten the ageing period; it is required for proper stabilizer hydration and a smoother churn.
  10. Stir the aged base thoroughly. Churn in a pre-chilled ice cream machine for 35–45 minutes, or until the ice cream has a dense, soft-serve consistency.
  11. Transfer immediately to a chilled storage container. Press parchment or food-safe film directly onto the surface and seal with a lid.
  12. Harden at −18°C for at least four hours before serving. For the best scooping texture, allow the container to temper briefly at refrigeration temperature before portioning.

Production Notes

Flavour adjustment: Evaluate the ice cream only after it has fully hardened. Frozen products taste less intense than liquid bases. For the next batch, adjust the Flavor Fountain in small increments rather than adding extra sugar.

Colour target: Use a pale-to-medium bubble-gum pink. Excess colouring makes the product look artificial and can stain the mouth, which is undesirable for a family-focused product.

Do not substitute: LorAnn Super-Strength Cotton Candy Flavor is much more concentrated than the Ice Cream Flavor Fountain and must not be used at the quantity shown here.

Mix-ins: Avoid. The clean, smooth format gives the strongest flavour recognition and the most reliable frozen storage.

Storage: Keep tightly covered at −18°C. Best texture and aroma are expected within approximately two weeks.

Scaling: Multiply every ingredient by the same factor. Maintain the CMC ratio exactly; do not round it aggressively in larger batches.
