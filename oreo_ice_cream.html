Blue M Signature Oreo Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 475g 2% milk
  • 210g heavy cream (33%)
  • 95g granulated sugar
  • 60g corn syrup
  • 38g skim milk powder
  • 2.4g CMC powder
  • 3 large egg yolks (~50g)
  • 20g organic cocoa powder
  • 2g vanilla bean paste
  • 80g Oreo cookies (for infusion)
  • 120g Oreo cookies (crushed, fold-in)
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Oreo Cream Swirl

  • 60g Oreo cream filling (scraped from cookies)
  • 20g whole milk

Instructions

  1. Whisk together sugar, skim milk powder, cocoa powder, CMC powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and Oreo cookies for infusion. Heat gently and hold for 10 minutes to extract flavor.
  3. Strain out Oreo solids, pressing lightly to extract flavor.
  4. Add dry mix and continue heating until fully dissolved.
  5. Whisk egg yolks in a separate bowl. Temper with warm dairy, then return to the saucepan.
  6. Cook while stirring constantly until the custard thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon.
  7. Remove from heat and blend in vanilla bean paste until fully emulsified.
  8. Strain through a fine mesh sieve and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  9. Refrigerate for 12 hours to mature the base.
  10. Prepare Oreo cream swirl by whisking cream filling with milk until smooth and slightly pourable. Chill.
  11. Churn ice cream until soft-serve consistency.
  12. Layer ice cream with Oreo cream swirl and crushed Oreos as you pack into a freezer-safe container, creating ribbons and pockets throughout.
  13. Harden at -18°C for at least 4 hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~260

Notes

A premium cookies and cream ice cream designed for maximum impact. Oreo flavor is layered in three dimensions: infused into the base, folded in as chunks, and swirled as a creamy ribbon. The result is a balanced, high-end dessert with deep chocolate notes, smooth texture, and striking visual contrast. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 7–10 days. Serve at -13°C for optimal texture.
