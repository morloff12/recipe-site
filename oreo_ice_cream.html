Blue M Signature Oreo Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 475g 2% milk
  • 210g heavy cream (33%)
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 60g corn syrup
  • 38g skim milk powder
  • 2.4g CMC powder
  • 3 large egg yolks (~50g)
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • 120g Oreo cookies (crushed, fold-in)
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Oreo Cream Swirl

  • 60g Oreo cream filling (scraped)
  • 25g whole milk

Oreo Chocolate Swirl (Optional)

  • 10g organic cocoa powder
  • 35g granulated sugar
  • 50g heavy cream
  • 30g Oreo cookies (finely crushed, infused)

Instructions

  1. Whisk together sugar, skim milk powder, CMC powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat gently while stirring to 60–65°C until fully dissolved.
  3. Whisk egg yolks in a separate bowl. Temper with some warm dairy, then return to the saucepan.
  4. Cook while stirring constantly to 82–84°C until the custard thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Remove from heat and blend in vanilla bean paste until fully emulsified.
  6. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  7. Refrigerate for 12 hours to mature the base.
  8. Prepare Oreo cream swirl by whisking cream filling with milk until smooth and slightly pourable. Chill.
  9. For Oreo chocolate swirl, whisk cocoa powder and sugar in a bowl. Heat cream to 60–65°C and pour over the cocoa mixture. Whisk until smooth, then mix in finely crushed Oreos. Cool to a thick but pourable consistency before use.
  10. Churn ice cream until soft-serve consistency.
  11. Layer ice cream with Oreo cream swirl, Oreo chocolate swirl, and crushed Oreos as you pack into a freezer-safe container. Do not overmix to preserve ribbons.
  12. Harden at -18°C for at least 4 hours before serving. Serve at -13°C for optimal texture.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~260

Notes

A refined cookies and cream ice cream built on a clean sweet cream base to highlight Oreo flavor without bitterness. Finished with crushed Oreos, a creamy filling swirl, and an optional Oreo chocolate ribbon for added depth. Smooth, balanced, and visually striking. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 7–10 days. Serve at -13°C.
