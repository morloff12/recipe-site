Rocky Road Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 500g 2% milk
  • 150g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 70g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 40g organic cocoa powder
  • 60g semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 50g marshmallow fluff
  • 30g chopped roasted pecans
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • 1g fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together sugar, skim milk powder, cocoa powder, salt, and CMC powder.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  3. Add chocolate chips and vanilla bean paste. Blend using an immersion blender until smooth and emulsified.
  4. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  5. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  6. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  7. Fold in marshmallow fluff and chopped roasted pecans in streaks to retain texture.
  8. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~240

Notes

A nostalgic blend of chocolate, marshmallow, and roasted pecans, this Rocky Road ice cream is rich, nutty, and swirled with gooey sweetness. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -13°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
