Cherry Garcia Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 500g 2% milk
  • 150g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 70g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 120g frozen dark sweet cherries
  • 15g sugar (for cherry reduction)
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • 40g mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine cherries and 15g sugar. Simmer gently for 10–15 minutes until thickened. Cool and refrigerate.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together granulated sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder.
  3. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and the dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C, stirring constantly.
  4. Add vanilla bean paste. Blend with immersion blender until smooth.
  5. Strain mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container. Cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  7. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  8. Fold in reduced cherries and mini chocolate chips.
  9. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~215

Notes

A creamy cherry ice cream with swirls of reduced sweet cherries and flecks of dark chocolate, balancing rich fruit and indulgent crunch. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -13°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
