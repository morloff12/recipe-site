Brownie Batter Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 500g 2% milk
  • 150g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 70g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 40g organic cocoa powder
  • Prepared gluten-free brownie batter (made from store-bought mix, chilled)
  • 50g semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the brownie batter according to the directions on your gluten-free mix box. To ensure a rich, chewy texture that won't dry out in the ice cream, underbake the brownie batter slightly—aim to remove it from the oven when the center is still glossy and jiggles slightly. Let cool completely in the pan, then chill in the refrigerator until firm. Scoop or cut into bite-sized chunks and keep cold until ready to fold into the churned ice cream.
  2. Whisk together granulated sugar, skim milk powder, cocoa powder, CMC powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  4. Add vanilla bean paste. Blend with immersion blender until smooth.
  5. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  7. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  8. Fold in chunks of prepared gluten-free brownie batter and semi-sweet chocolate chips.
  9. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~240

Notes

This rich and decadent ice cream swirls chunks of chilled gluten-free brownie batter and chocolate chips into a creamy cocoa base. Completely gluten-free and dangerously indulgent. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -13°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
