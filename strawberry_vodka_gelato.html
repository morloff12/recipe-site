Strawberry Vodka Gelato

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 500g 2% milk
  • 150g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 70g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 150g fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 10g sugar (for berry reduction)
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • 8g vodka (Grey Goose preferred)

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, cook the strawberries and berry reduction sugar until thickened and syrupy. Cool and purée. Refrigerate until needed.
  2. Whisk together granulated sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder in a bowl.
  3. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  4. Add vanilla bean paste and vodka. Blend with immersion blender until smooth.
  5. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  7. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  8. Fold in strawberry purée before hardening.
  9. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~205

Notes

This silky strawberry gelato is folded with real puréed berries and kissed with a splash of vodka for a vibrant, grown-up twist. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -13°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
