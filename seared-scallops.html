Seared Scallops with Blood Orange and Smoked Paprika Sauce

Serves: 4

Time: PT30M

Whole wheat couscous and steamed or grilled vegetables are great additions to the scallops for a complete meal. Blood orange juice adds vibrant flavor to the sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup blood orange juice
  • 16 sea scallops
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ground cumin, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Heat blood orange juice in a medium saucepan over low heat.
  2. Simmer until reduced by half, about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once juice is almost reduced, season scallops with salt, pepper and cumin on both sides.
  4. Heat a large cast-iron skillet over high heat until smoking.
  5. Add oil to skillet and sear scallops, about 1 to 2 minutes per side, until golden-brown and just cooked through.
  6. While scallops are searing, whisk butter into the warm reduced orange juice.
  7. Continue whisking until sauce is shiny and smooth.
  8. Whisk in smoked paprika, taste, and adjust seasoning as needed.
  9. Spoon a bit of sauce onto each serving plate, top with scallops, and serve immediately.
