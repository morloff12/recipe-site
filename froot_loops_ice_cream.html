Froot Loops Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 475g 2% milk
  • 210g heavy cream (33%)
  • 105g granulated sugar
  • 57g corn syrup
  • 38g skim milk powder
  • 2.4g CMC powder
  • 3 large egg yolks (~50g)
  • 4g LorAnn Fruity Cereal Milk Flavor Oil
  • 50g Froot Loops cereal (crushed, for infusion)
  • 30g Froot Loops cereal (lightly crushed, to fold in after churning)
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk together sugar, skim milk powder, CMC powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, crushed Froot Loops (infusion portion), and dry mix. Heat to 60–65°C, stirring until sugars dissolve and cereal flavor infuses (about 10 minutes). Strain out cereal solids.
  3. Whisk the egg yolks in a separate bowl. Temper with some of the warm dairy mixture, then return to the pot.
  4. Continue heating the custard base to 82–84°C while stirring constantly.
  5. Remove from heat and blend in LorAnn Fruity Cereal Milk Flavor Oil with an immersion blender until fully emulsified.
  6. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  7. Refrigerate for 12 hours to mature the base and fully hydrate stabilizers.
  8. Churn in a compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  9. Fold in lightly crushed Froot Loops. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving. Serve at -13°C for optimal scoopability.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~240

Notes

A creamy cereal-infused ice cream bursting with the nostalgic flavor of Froot Loops. Features both an infused base and crunchy bits folded in after churning for texture. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 7 days for peak crunch.
