Mojito Mint Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 475g 2% milk
  • 210g heavy cream (33%)
  • 105g granulated sugar
  • 57g corn syrup
  • 38g skim milk powder
  • 2.4g CMC powder
  • 3 large egg yolks (~50g)
  • 15g fresh mojito mint leaves (washed)
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 40ml white rum
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Whisk together granulated sugar, skim milk powder, CMC powder, and fine sea salt in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 60–65°C, stirring until sugars dissolve.
  3. Add mint leaves and lime zest. Simmer gently for 5 minutes to infuse, then remove from heat and let steep for an additional 10 minutes.
  4. Strain out mint leaves and zest, pressing gently to extract flavor.
  5. Whisk egg yolks in a separate bowl. Temper with some of the warm dairy mixture, then return to the pot.
  6. Continue heating the custard base to 82–84°C while stirring constantly.
  7. Remove from heat and blend in lime juice and white rum with an immersion blender until fully emulsified.
  8. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  9. Refrigerate for 12 hours to mature the base and fully hydrate stabilizers.
  10. Churn in a compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  11. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving. Serve at -13°C for optimal scoopability.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~235

Notes

A refreshing twist on a cocktail classic, this ice cream blends the fresh taste of mojito mint leaves, bright lime zest, and the subtle warmth of white rum into a creamy base. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best enjoyed within 5–7 days for peak mint freshness.
