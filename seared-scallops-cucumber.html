Seared Scallops with Mojito-Lime Gastrique, Mint Oil, and Compressed Cucumber

Serves: 4

Prep Time: 25 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

Scallops

  • 12 large dry sea scallops
  • Sea salt and white pepper
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil (grapeseed or canola)
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Mojito-Lime Gastrique

  • 1/4 cup white rum
  • 2 tbsp white balsamic or rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp lime zest
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4–6 mojito mint leaves, torn

Mint Oil

  • 1/2 cup olive oil (light or extra virgin)
  • 1/4 cup mojito mint leaves (blanched)
  • Pinch of sea salt

Compressed Cucumber & Garnish

  • 1 cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced or compressed using vacuum method
  • Fresh mint chiffonade
  • Microgreens (optional)
  • Lime zest
  • Crushed pink peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Blanch mint leaves for 10 seconds, shock in ice water, squeeze dry. Blend with olive oil and salt until smooth. Strain and reserve.
  2. Simmer sugar and vinegar until slightly syrupy. Add rum, lime juice, and zest. Reduce by half. Add mint, steep 2 minutes, strain and keep warm.
  3. Pat scallops dry and season. Sear in hot oil 1.5–2 minutes per side. Add butter and baste during last 30 seconds.
  4. Prepare cucumber: thinly slice or compress using vacuum sealer. Chill until ready to plate.
  5. To plate: arrange scallops with cucumber slices, drizzle mojito-lime gastrique and mint oil, and garnish with mint chiffonade, lime zest, microgreens, and pink peppercorns.
