Chili Lime Air Fryer Jumbo Shrimp

Bright citrus. Gentle heat. Fast air fryer cook. Built as a steakhouse side for bison, fondant potatoes, and asparagus.

Yield: 3 to 4 servings
Prep time: 20 minutes
Cook time: 6 minutes
Total time: 26 minutes

Ingredients

  • 12 to 16 jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined, tails on
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Zest of 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 tsp chili flakes, or finely minced fresh red chili
  • 1 small garlic clove, grated fine
  • 1 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 lime wedge, for serving

Instructions

  1. Pat shrimp dry with paper towel.
  2. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lime zest, lime juice, chili, garlic, and salt.
  3. Toss shrimp in the marinade. Rest 15 minutes at room temperature.
  4. Preheat air fryer to 400 F for 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Lightly oil the basket or tray.
  6. Arrange shrimp in a single layer with space between. Cook in batches if needed.
  7. Air fry 5 to 6 minutes total, flipping halfway, until opaque with light browning at the edges.
  8. Transfer shrimp to a bowl right away. Toss once with cilantro.
  9. Serve with a lime wedge on the side.

Notes

For best texture, do not marinate longer than 15 minutes. Acid can start to cure the shrimp. Optional doneness check: pull at 120 to 125 F internal.
