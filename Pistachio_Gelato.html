Pistachio Gelato

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 580g whole milk (3.25%)
  • 90g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 65g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 35g shelled toasted pistachios
  • 12g icing sugar
  • 0.3g fine sea salt
  • 2–3g neutral oil (optional, for blending)
  • 3g vanilla bean paste (preferred over extract for richer flavor and visual specks)

Instructions

  1. If using raw pistachios, toast at 160°C for 8–10 minutes until fragrant. If already toasted, proceed directly to blending.
  2. Cool slightly, then blend pistachios, icing sugar, and salt in a food processor until smooth. Add a small amount of oil if needed to emulsify. Set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder.
  4. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  5. Add pistachio paste and vanilla bean paste (if using). Emulsify using an immersion blender.
  6. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  7. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  8. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  9. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~220

Notes

Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -11°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
