Pistachio Gelato

Yield: ~1 Quart / ~950 mL
Serving Temperature: -11°C
Preparation Time: 12 hours (including chilling)
Author: Home Gelato Bible

Ingredients

  • 580g Whole Milk (3.25%)
  • 90g Heavy Cream (33%)
  • 90g Rogers Granulated Sugar (Sucrose)
  • 65g Crown Corn Syrup (Glucose Syrup)
  • 35g Skim Milk Powder
  • 2.5g CMC Powder (Stabilizer)
  • 50g Homemade Pistachio Paste (see preparation below)
  • 3g Vanilla Paste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast 35g shelled pistachios at 160°C for 8–10 min. Cool, then blend with 12g icing sugar and 0.3g fine sea salt until smooth. Add 2–3g neutral oil if needed. Set aside (~50g yield).
  2. In a bowl, whisk sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder together.
  3. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  4. Add pistachio paste and vanilla. Emulsify using an immersion blender.
  5. Strain base through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container. Chill rapidly to under 10°C.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  7. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (~-6.5°C).
  8. Pack into a 1 QT container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Servings: 10 (90 mL each)
Estimated Calories per Serving: ~220

Storage

Store at -18°C. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at serving temp (-11°C) before scooping.
