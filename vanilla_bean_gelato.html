Vanilla Bean Gelato

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 600g whole milk (3.25%)
  • 100g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 60g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 1 vanilla bean, split and scraped (or 5g vanilla bean paste)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together granulated sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and the dry mix. Add the scraped seeds and pod of the vanilla bean (or vanilla bean paste).
  3. Heat the mixture to 75–80°C, stirring constantly. Do not boil.
  4. Remove vanilla pod and blend using an immersion blender until fully emulsified.
  5. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  7. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  8. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~200

Notes

This smooth and classic gelato delivers rich vanilla flavor with delicate specks of real vanilla bean. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -11°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
