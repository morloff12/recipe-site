Chili Lime Jumbo Shrimp

Pan fried or air fryer chili lime jumbo shrimp with smoked paprika, real lime juice, and a mild sweet heat glaze. Perfect with bison steak, sweet potato fries, and asparagus.

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 907 g jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • 4 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 2 tbsp sweet chili sauce
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Pat shrimp completely dry.
  2. Toss shrimp with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, chili flakes, and lime zest. Rest 10 minutes.
  3. Heat skillet over medium high. Add olive oil.
  4. Sear shrimp 90 seconds to 2 minutes per side until opaque. Work in batches. Remove from pan.
  5. Reduce heat to medium. Add butter and garlic. Cook 20 seconds.
  6. Add lime juice, sweet chili sauce, and honey. Simmer 30 seconds.
  7. Return shrimp to pan. Toss 30 to 45 seconds until glazed.
  8. Remove from heat. Finish with parsley and serve.

Air Fryer Option

  1. Toss shrimp with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, chili flakes, and olive oil only.
  2. Air fry at 400 F for 5 to 6 minutes, shaking once, until just opaque.
  3. Meanwhile melt butter in skillet. Add garlic and cook 20 seconds.
  4. Add lime zest, lime juice, sweet chili sauce, and honey. Simmer 30 seconds.
  5. Toss hot shrimp in glaze for 30 seconds. Finish with parsley.
