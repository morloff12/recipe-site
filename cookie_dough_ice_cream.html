Cookie Dough Ice Cream

Yield: 950 grams
Preparation Time: PT1H
Cook Time: PT40M
Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 500g 2% milk
  • 150g heavy cream (33%)
  • 90g granulated sugar
  • 70g corn syrup
  • 35g skim milk powder
  • 2.5g CMC powder
  • 3g vanilla bean paste
  • 50g mini chocolate chips (semi-sweet)

Gluten-Free Cookie Dough

  • 35g Bob's Red Mill 1-to-1 Gluten Free Flour
  • 20g brown sugar
  • 15g granulated sugar
  • 20g unsalted butter, softened
  • 1g vanilla bean paste
  • 15g mini chocolate chips (semi-sweet)
  • Pinch of fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. To make the cookie dough, mix together butter, sugars, vanilla paste, salt, and flour. Fold in chocolate chips. Form small chunks and freeze until needed.
  2. Whisk together granulated sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder in a bowl.
  3. In a saucepan, combine milk, cream, corn syrup, and dry mix. Heat to 75–80°C while stirring.
  4. Add vanilla bean paste and blend with immersion blender until smooth.
  5. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a clean container and cool rapidly to under 10°C.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–12 hours to mature the base.
  7. Churn in compressor ice cream maker until soft-serve consistency (approximately 35–45 minutes depending on machine and starting temperature).
  8. Fold in frozen cookie dough chunks and mini chocolate chips.
  9. Pack into a freezer-safe container and harden in the freezer at -18°C for 4+ hours before serving.

Nutrition

Serving Size: 95 grams
Calories per Serving: ~240

Notes

Chunks of gluten-free chocolate chip cookie dough and mini chips are folded into a classic vanilla base for the perfect nostalgic treat. Store at -18°C in an airtight container. Best consumed within 10 days. Let sit at -13°C for 5–10 minutes before serving.
