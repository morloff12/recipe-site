Tawny Port Ice Cream with Fig Swirl

A rich custard ice cream infused with aged tawny port, delivering deep notes of caramel, dried fruit, and oak. Finished with a concentrated fig swirl for contrast, texture, and brightness. Designed for a smooth, scoopable texture straight from the freezer with a refined, adult-forward profile. Gluten-free.

Yield: ~980g

Author: Blue M Ice Cream & Gelato

Ingredients

  • 2% milk, 500 g
  • Heavy cream (33%), 220 g
  • Granulated sugar, 90 g
  • Light corn syrup, 60 g
  • Skim milk powder, 45 g
  • Egg yolks, 90 g
  • CMC powder, 2.5 g
  • Fine sea salt, 1.5 g
  • Tawny port (reduced), 80 g
  • Vanilla bean paste, 5 g

Fig Swirl

  • Dried figs, 180 g
  • Water, 120 g
  • Granulated sugar, 30 g
  • Lemon juice, 10 g
  • Fine sea salt, 0.5 g

Instructions

  1. Combine granulated sugar, skim milk powder, and CMC powder.
  2. Heat milk, cream, and corn syrup to 60–65°C.
  3. Whisk egg yolks separately.
  4. Temper egg yolks with warm dairy mixture.
  5. Return mixture to heat and cook to 82–84°C.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Add reduced tawny port and vanilla bean paste.
  8. Blend with immersion blender until fully smooth.
  9. Strain through fine mesh sieve.
  10. Cool mixture to below 10°C.
  11. Age base for 12 hours.
  12. Churn for 35–45 minutes.
  13. Layer ice cream with fig swirl during packing.
  14. Harden at -18°C.

Fig Swirl Instructions

  1. Combine figs, water, sugar, lemon juice, and salt.
  2. Simmer until figs are fully softened.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Reduce gently until thick and spreadable.
  5. Cool completely before use.
